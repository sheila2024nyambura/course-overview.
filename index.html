Artificial Intelligence for Banking Marketing: Strategic Application Workshop

Course Overview

Artificial Intelligence is reshaping how banks approach marketing, decision-making, and customer engagement. This two-day workshop is designed to give participants the skills and insights needed to thrive in an AI-driven world. Through practical exercises, expert presentations, and case studies, attendees will explore how AI can create real value in the financial sector. The course blends technical fundamentals with strategic applications, making it suitable for both managers and specialists. By the end of the program, participants will not only understand AI concepts but also know how to apply them in their daily work. The training concludes with a recognized AI Basics Certification that validates new competencies.

Course Objectives

  • Provide a solid understanding of AI fundamentals and tools.
  • Showcase practical AI applications in banking and marketing.
  • Equip participants with decision-making strategies using AI insights.
  • Encourage innovation and ethical considerations in AI adoption.
  • Prepare teams for AI certification and future-ready skills.

Target Audience

The program is designed for marketing professionals in banks and financial institutions, including managers, analysts, specialists, and strategists. It is also open to employees in customer service, data management, HR, operations, and other departments who wish to build future-ready AI competencies.

Training Approach & Format

A two-day interactive workshop featuring expert presentations, case studies, and group exercises. The course will cover AI fundamentals, ethical considerations, and real-world banking applications. Participants will receive a recognized AI Basics Certification upon completion.

Expected Outcomes

  • Strong understanding of AI concepts, tools, and their applications.
  • Ability to apply AI in daily work to improve efficiency and decision-making.
  • Development of strategies for AI adoption and innovation in banking marketing.

Key Benefits for Banks

  • Deeper customer engagement through personalized marketing.
  • Improved operational efficiency and maximized ROI.
  • Teams equipped with certified, future-ready AI skills.
